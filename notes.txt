STAFF_MASTER
Records of current and past staff members at the organisation.

staff_id: the unique ID for a staff member
department: the department the staff member works for
grade: the grade for the staff member's role - higher grades are more senior
contract_type: whether the staff member is on a permanent or fixed-term contract
fte: the proportion of full-time hours worked by the staff member ie 1.0 for a staff member working full-time, less than 1.0 for staff members who work part-time. Note that salaries are proportionate to this value for part-time staff
start_date: the date the staff member began working in their role
last_promotion_date: the date the staff member was last promoted
gender: the staff member's gender
ethnicity: the staff member's ethnicity
exit_date: the date the staff member left the organisation


GRADE_SALARIES
Maps jobs grades to the salaries paid by roles at that grade

grade: the job grade
salary: the salary paid by jobs at that grade
